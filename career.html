Computational Chemistry


COMPUTATIONAL CHEMISTRY JOBS

There are many job careers under the field of Computational Chemistry. However, there are specifications in the duties depending on the specific subfield of computational chemistry choice. For example, on the development side, it is focused on creating and testing new statistical and mathematical packages and algorithms that may have applications in numerous fields. In applied computational chemistry, it requires theoretical knowledge to tackle real-world problems, and the duties include experiment design, data collection, publication of research, and etc.

Scientist Computational Chemistry

Scientists specialized in computational chemistry that applies computer-based techniques to understand and solve chemical problems.

Pharmaceutical Computational Chemistry


Pharmaceutical scientists specialized in computational chemistry that focuses on the application of computational techniques to solve problems related to drug discovery, design, and development.

Computational Designer


Computational designers specialized in computational chemistry that deals with leveraging computational methods, algorithms, and software to generate, analyze, and optimize designs.

Computational Quantum Chemistry


Quantum chemists specialized in computational chemistry that focuses on using quantum mechanics to understand and predict the behavior of molecules and chemical reactions.
